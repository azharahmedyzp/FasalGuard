Foliage Imaging Best Practices

  • Ensure leaves are well-lit and in sharp optical focus.
  • Center the infected or symptomatic sections of the leaf.
  • Avoid high motion blur, shadow cast, or direct solar glare.
  • Capture images using natural, diffused lighting where possible.
